About

Neon, The Film Club of IIIT Kota aims to introduce students to the various aspects of the popular entertainment medium, cinema and focuses on the informal gatherings where themes, experiences and emotions that form part of our lives are discussed. If you are interested not only in watching movies but also in learning how to create them, attend a meeting of the club. With Neon, you can explore your abilities in many fields of the film industry, such as: writing, directing, producing, acting, film editing and budgeting. The club and its endeavors are entirely student run.
